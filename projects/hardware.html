Robotic Leaf Grasping Manipulator

A custom manipulator capable of grasping crop leaves and applying a reflectance spectrometer to the leaf's surface to collect rapid, autonomous spectral reflectance scans. This manipulator is mounted to the mobile robotic base developed previous on the project I collaborated on for my Master's project at CMU.

Field Reflectance Spectrometer

A portable reflectance spectrometer system capable of being mounted to an autonomous robotic system to take rapid spectral measurements of the stalks and leaves of various crops. This device was developed as part of my Master Research in Robotics at CMU.
